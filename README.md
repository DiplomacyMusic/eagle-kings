# 🦅 EAGLE KINGS

**Five friends. Five eagles. One living room.**

A SNES-style Street Fighter homage starring one extremely real photograph. Play it here:

**https://diplomacymusic.github.io/eagle-kings/**

Works in any browser. On iPhone: open the link in Safari, rotate to landscape, and tap **Share → Add to Home Screen** for fullscreen.

## The Roster

| Fighter | Special | Style |
|---|---|---|
| **VERA** — The Falconess | LOVE TALON | Balanced. Her eagle flies point-blank into your business. |
| **SHADES** — Too Cool to Flinch | SHADE DIVE | Heavy hitter. His eagle dive-bombs from the ceiling. |
| **ROOST** — The Calm Center | WING WALL | Tank. His eagle orbits him like a feathered moat. |
| **SCREECH** — !! It Lives on His Head !! | SONIC SCREECH | Speedster. The eagle on his head screams people over. |
| **HUH?** — Even the Eagle Wonders | WHO KNOWS? | Trickster. Vanishes, reappears behind you. Eagle included. |

## Controls

**Touch (phone):** D-pad to move, drag up to jump, down to crouch. PUNCH / KICK / EAGLE buttons. Hold back to block. Tap menus to navigate.

**Keyboard P1:** WASD move · F punch · G kick · H eagle special
**Keyboard P2:** Arrows move · `,` punch · `.` kick · `/` special
Hold **back** to block · **P** pause · **M** mute

Land hits to fill your meter — when it reads **EAGLE READY!**, unleash the bird.

Best of 3 rounds. Feathers will fly.
